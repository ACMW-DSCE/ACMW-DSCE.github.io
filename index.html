About Us

Our vision

ACM-W supports, celebrates and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field. We at ACM-W DSCE, wish to encourage women at every step in their contribution towards technology. We provide a huge platform for ideas of the young minds to manifest and take form.ACM-W is an atmosphere where women can learn and succeed.

Our mission

We at ACM-W DSCE make it our solitary goal to empower women to unveil their full potential, realise their self-worth and influence significant social changes with their contributions towards STEM, by providing them with a positive, constructive and beneficial workspace. We engage in various activities throughout the year through webinars, seminars and several interactive events to keep them involved, motivated and help them improve their skillset. Women at ACM-W DSCE can dream, aspire and achieve their goals by grasping countless opportunities they're presented with.
